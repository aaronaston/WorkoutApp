# Strength - Hinge/Push

## Prep
- Hamstring Lunge Rocks — 1 x 60–90s per side
- Deep Lunge with Sky (Thoracic) Reaches — 1 x 60–90s per side
- Cat-Cow — 1 x 60–90s
- Lateral Banded Kicks — 2 x 15 per side
- Deadbug – Contralateral — 2 x 10 slow alternating
- Tempo Scap Push Ups — 2 x 5 (3s per direction)

## Practice: Balance / Stability
- Flamingo Stand – Eyes Closed — 2 x 20–30s per side
- Back Scale — 2 x 40s per side

## Push
- Push Up Negative with Deficit — 3–4 x 6
- Side Plank Reaches — 3–4 x 12 per side
- B-Stance Hip Thrust — 3–4 x 12 per side
- Pallof Press — 3–4 x 12; start making harder by increasing resistance
- Power Step Up — 3–4 x 12 per side
- Hex Press — 3–4 x 12

## Ponder / Cool-Down
- Foam Roll – Hamstrings — 1 x 90s per side
- Short BW 1 — 1 x 90s per side
