# Strength - Hinge/Push

- Deep Lunge with Sky (Thoracic) Reaches — 1 x 60s per side
- Tail Wags — 1 x 60s
- Deadbug – Contralateral — 2 x 12 slow alternating
- Tempo Scap Push Ups — 2 x 6 (2–3s per direction)
- Single Leg Glute Bridge with Wall — 2 x 12 per side

- Dumbbell Floor Press — 3–4 x 12
- Kneeling Pallof Press with Rotation — 3–4 x 12 per side
- Elevated Heel Taps – Foot Back (Hip Focused) — 3–4 x 12 per side
