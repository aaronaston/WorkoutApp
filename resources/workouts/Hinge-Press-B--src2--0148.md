# Hinge/Press B

## Prep
- Adductor Rocks — 1 x ~15, per side
- Roll to Pike — 1 x 10
- Puppy Stretch (Alternating) — 1 x ~10-15; small shifts/rocks
- Laying Pelvic Tilt — 1 x ~15
- Deadbug with Knee Press — 2 x 8, per side
- Prone W with Reach — 2 x 8; option to hold small plates
- Back Scales — 2 x 30-40s, per side

## Push 1
- Romanian Deadlift with Barbell or Dumbbells — 3 x 8-10; per side, focus on core + glutes (15# DBs, kickstands noted)
- Front Plate Hold — 3 x 30s

## Push 2
- Body Weight Glute Focus Step Ups — 3 x 8-10, per side; hold onto something for support, core braced
- Half Kneeling Single Arm DB Press — 3 x 8-10, per side; build in weight for challenge
- Crossbody Mountain Climber — 3 x 30; focus on control

## Ponder / Cool-Down
- Anchored T-Spine Opener — 1 x 60s+
- Foam Roll - Hamstrings OR Glute Stretch of Choice — 1 x 60s+, per side
