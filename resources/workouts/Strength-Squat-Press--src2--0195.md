# Strength - Squat/Press

- Froggy Hip Opener Rocks — 1 x 15
- Side Lying Thoracic Reaches — 1 x 5 per side
- Laying Windshield Wiper – Stretch — 1 x 10 alternating; no lower back lifting like shown
- Lateral Banded Monster Walk — 2–3 x 15 per direction
- Deadbug – Contralateral — 2–3 x 10 alternating slowly
- Leg Swings – Psoas Release — 2–3 x 15 per side

## Practice: Colt Twist & Frogger (~3 minutes)
- Colt Twist + Frogger

## Push 1
- Bent Arm Bear (Low) — 3 x 10–12 steps
- Front Foot Elevated Split Squat — 3 x 10–12 per side

## Push 2: EMOM 12 or 16 with focus on quality

- Plank Slides with Weight — 3–4 x 40s
- Dumbbell Front Squat — 3–4 x 40s (option to squat to box if reduces pain)

- Foam Rolling – Quads — 1 x 60–90s per side
- Lat-Tricep Bench Stretch — 1 x 90s
