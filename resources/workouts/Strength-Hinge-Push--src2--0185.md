# Strength - Hinge/Push

## Prep
- Hamstring Lunge Rocks — 1 x 60–90s per side
- Deep Lunge with Sky (Thoracic) Reaches — 1 x 60–90s per side
- Cat-Cow — 1 x 60–90s
- Side Lying Hip Abduction — 2 x 15 per side
- Deadbug – Contralateral — 2 x 10 slow alternating
- Tempo Scap Push Ups — 2 x 5 (3s per direction)

## Practice: Balance & Stability (~3–5 minutes)

- Hip Airplane

## Push

- Dumbbell Floor Press — 3 x 10
- Elevated Push Up — 3–4 x 12
- Kneeling Pallof Press with Rotation — 3–4 x 12 per side
- Elevated Heel Taps – Foot Back (Hip Focused) — 3–4 x 12 per side

## Ponder / Cool-Down
- Foam Roll – Hamstrings — 1 x 90s per side
- Short BW 1 — 1 x 90s per side
