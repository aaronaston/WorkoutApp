# Hinge/Press

## Prep
- Adductor Rocks — 1 x ~15 per side
- Roll to Pike — 1 x 10
- Puppy Stretch (Alternating) — 1 x ~10–15 small shifts/rocks
- Laying Pelvic Tilt — 1 x ~15
- Deadbug Pallof Press — 2 x 10
- Prone Y-T-Ws — 2 x 6–8 lifts per position
- Flamingo Stand — 2 x 30s per side; consider eyes closed

## Push 1
- Single Leg Posted Romanian Deadlift — 3 x 8–10 per side; build in weight if moving well (Set 1: 20/25/30)
- Dumbbell Strict Press — 3 x 8–10; build in weight if moving well (Set 1: 20#)
- Kneeling Banded Woodchoppers — 3 x 12–15 per side; increase resistance for challenge

## Push 2: Intervals 30s on / 10s off
- Dumbbell Hang Power Clean — 4 x 30s
- Dumbbell Push Press — 4 x 30s
- Copenhagen Plank – Right — 4 x 30s
- Copenhagen Plank – Left — 4 x 30s
- Rest Station — 4 x 30s

## Ponder / Cool Down
- Anchored T-Spine Opener — 1 x 60s+
- Foam Roll – Hamstrings OR Glute Stretch of Choice — 1 x 60s+ per side
