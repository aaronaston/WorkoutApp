# Push/Single Leg B

## Prep
- Banded Dislocates — 1 x 15–20
- Thread-the-Needle with Sky Reach — 3 x 8–10 per side
- 90/90s with Raise — 1 x 10 alternating
- Laying Pelvic Tilt — 1 x 10–15 (slow with breath)
- Scap CAR with Yoga Block — 2 x 6–8 per direction
- Side Plank Clamshells with Band — 2 x 15 per side
- Glute Bridge with Adductor Squeeze — 2 x 15

## Practice: Balance/Stability
- Standing Pelvic Stability Drills — 2 x ~30s per side
- Single Leg Long Jumps — explore as desired (accumulate 10–15 per side)

## Push
- Dumbbell Bench Press – Neutral — 3 x 10–12
- Body Weight Glute Focus Step Ups — 3 x 8–10 per side; option to hold onto something for support
- Plank Slides with Weight — 3 x 20
- Active Bottom of Push Up Hold — 3 x 30s
- Seated Hip Flexor Lifts – Single Leg — 3 x 30s per side

## Ponder / Stretch
- Forward Splits — 1 x 90s per side
- Childs Pose with Reaches — 1 x 90s
